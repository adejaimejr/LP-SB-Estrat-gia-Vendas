# SB VENDAS — Landing Page Prompt for Replit Design Mode

I am building a premium landing page for **SB Vendas — Estratégias & Vendas**, the personal brand of **Samara Benevides**, a Brazilian expert in sales strategy, commercial consulting, WhatsApp automation, CRM implementation, conversational flows, AI agents, and team training. 

The page should position Samara as a premium authority in sales optimization — she's not selling software, she's selling her expertise and hands-on consulting. The design must feel like a **luxury consulting brand** that happens to use cutting-edge technology.

**Design Reference:** Custklen — CRM Services Landing Page by Plainthing Studio (Dribbble) — dark theme, clean sections, modern SaaS aesthetic, but adapted for a personal brand expert. See the attached `design.json` for complete style guidelines.

**Language:** All content must be in **Brazilian Portuguese (pt-BR)**.

**Important:** The hero section features a real photo of Samara — use the provided image (woman in professional attire against a dark background). Throughout the page, use her photos in relevant sections.
